About Me

I am pursuing a Master's Degree in Data Science at University of Michigan - Dearborn (expected May 2027) with a cumulative GPA of 4.0.

I have 3+ years of professional experience in Data Science and Analytics before moving to the United States to pursue my Master’s. My most recent role was Data Analyst at Gearlaunch, a global US-based company in the e-commerce and print-on-demand industry, where I was the sole data analyst responsible for the entire company’s analytics system. Prior to that, I worked at VCCORP, Vietnam’s leading tech company with the largest digital media ecosystem, first as a Data Analyst and later promoted to Data Analyst - Team Lead for a team of four, responsible for analytical growth of 7 media sites.

I specialize in analytic disciplines, including designing statistical experiments, building predictive models, deploying automated dashboards (Tableau, Google Cloud Platform), and implementing data-driven solutions such as recommendation systems and web scraping pipelines. My technical skill set includes Python, R, SQL, Tableau, Google Cloud, and Machine Learning. With my Master’s specialized in Computational Intelligence, I am sharpening my skills in advanced AI and Machine Learning, including Neural Networks, Deep Learning, Large Language Models, as well as full-cycle data product deployment.

I'm a enthusiastic learner (I built this portfolio website entirely on my own, with ZERO experience in CSS and JS!). I self-taught myself into a career in data and landed my first Data Analyst job when I was third-year undergraduate business student, then got promoted to Data Analyst - Team Lead just one month after graduation. With an incoming Master’s Degree in Data Science, I am eager to continue bringing curiosity, discipline, and drive to every project and grow as a data science professional.
